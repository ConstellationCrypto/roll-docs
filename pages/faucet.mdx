# Receive Faucet Funds

 To request funds from the Form testnet faucet: 
 1. Navigate to our [Faucet Page](https://info.form.network/faucet)
 2. Enter your wallet address
 3. Click "Request Tokens"

 After a few seconds, some testnet tokens will be deposited in your wallet!
